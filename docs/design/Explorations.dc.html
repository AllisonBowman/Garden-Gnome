GROUNDED IN THE REPO
Species windows + care-log gap math come straight from advisor.py (hold off / in window / past window). Sample data is the real seed catalog: Monstera 7–12d water, Calathea 60–80% RH, Snake Plant 14–21d. Palettes reuse the app green #2D6A4F and the landing page's ink/paper/marigold/clay.
DECISIONS LOCKED FROM YOUR ANSWERS
Environments are the dashboard, plants nested inside. One reasoning component reused everywhere (inline, sheet, jobs, chat). Forecast is a first-class factor, passed through each environment's lens (indoor / shelter / exposure modulate raw weather). Confidence = numeric + in-character hedge. Jobs: rolling 7 days, urgent floats up, day-pinned by weather. Gnome inferred from behavior and evolves — archetypes: Meticulous Botanist · Weekend Tender · Rescue Romantic · Wild Experimenter. Affiliate recs appear only where a recommendation creates the need.
ACCESSIBILITY RULE
No meaning carried by color alone, ever: direction = position + arrow + signed number; status = shape + label; charts use bands, hatching and annotations. Text contrast ≥ 4.5:1 in every direction below.
