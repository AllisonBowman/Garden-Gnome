Every plant lives somewhere

In PlantAdvocate, each plant belongs to a growing environment.

This demo is a playful version of a real idea in the app: your plants aren't a flat list — they live in environments, each with its own conditions. A sunny windowsill, a shaded balcony, a greenhouse, a community plot. A plant can even move between them and keep its full history intact, so its care always reflects where it actually lives.
