Privacy

Your data, plainly explained.

Effective Date: July 19, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how PlantAdvocate ("the App," "we," "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the App. PlantAdvocate is operated by Allison Bowman, a sole proprietor, based in the United States.

If you have questions about this policy or your data, contact us at support@plantadvocate.ai.

Information We Collect

Account Information. You sign in to PlantAdvocate with your Apple or Google account. When you do, we receive your name and email address from the provider you choose (Apple users may keep their email address private using Apple's private relay, and that choice is respected). We also create an account identifier that keeps your plants, care logs, and growing environments tied to your account. Account information is used only to operate the App — never for marketing or tracking.

Plant and Care Data. When you use PlantAdvocate, you provide information about your plants: nicknames, species selections, and a log of care activities (watering, fertilizing, repotting, and similar events). This data is stored so the App can track your plants and generate care recommendations.

Photos. If you use the App's photo-based species identification or plant diagnosis features, you grant the App access to your device's camera or photo library to capture or select an image. Photos you submit are transmitted to our servers for processing and are used solely to provide identification or diagnosis results.

Anonymized Community Data. PlantAdvocate may share aggregated, anonymized care and scheduling data (for example, general watering intervals across many users of a given species) to help improve care recommendations across the community. This data is stripped of information that could identify you individually before it is shared or aggregated. Anonymized community data sharing is off by default and happens only if you opt in within the App.

Technical Data. Like most apps, we may automatically receive limited technical information (such as error logs) needed to keep the App running reliably.

AI Processing

PlantAdvocate uses artificial intelligence to provide species identification, plant diagnosis, and care advice. Photos and plant details you submit for these features may be processed by third-party AI service providers on our behalf, solely to generate your results. These providers are not permitted to use your submissions for advertising. AI-generated results are informational and may contain errors; they are not a substitute for professional horticultural advice, and should never be relied on to determine whether a plant is safe to eat or touch. You can report inaccurate or inappropriate AI results directly in the App.

How We Use Information

We use the information described above to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's core features, including plant tracking, care logging, and care advice
  • Process photos you submit for species identification or diagnosis
  • Improve the accuracy of care recommendations using aggregated, anonymized data
  • Diagnose and fix technical problems

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on servers operated by our hosting provider and encrypted at rest. We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your individually identifiable plant or care data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. We may share anonymized, aggregated data as described above. We may also share information with service providers who help us operate the App (such as our hosting provider and AI service providers), solely for that purpose.

Children's Privacy

PlantAdvocate is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Your Rights and Choices

You can delete your account and all associated data — your plants, care logs, environments, and account information — at any time directly in the App (Settings → Delete Account). Deletion takes effect immediately. If you prefer, emailing us at the address above remains an alternative way to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data.

You may also decline camera or photo access at the device level, though this will limit the App's photo-based features.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
